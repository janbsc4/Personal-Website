SEO

Keyword

Research

Find the best and most obtainable keywords


Head-Term keyword: 1-2 word keyword: “new books”

Long-tail: 3+ keywords: “new science fiction books”

Keyword with intent: “buy”

CPC * Traffic * Intent gives you an idea how valuable a keyword

Use to justify how much money SEO is saving you


Process

  1. Brainstorm all the possible Keywords (30min-2hours)
  2. Use Google Adwords to find the most valuable and obtainable ones
  3. Use Adwords Keyword Suggestion tool to look at the competing websites and find bad competition
  4. Prioritize the list of best keywords

Keyword Targeting

  • Put keywords naturally in your content
  • Blog allows you to target keywords naturally
  • Don’t use your chosen keyword more than 2-3 times per page. More might be treated as spam
  • Air on the side of caution, since it is hard to rehabilitate once google thinks your site is spam
  • Don’t duplicate content

    • On Page SEO

    Meta Tags

    HTML tags that can let search engines know details about your site. Plugins like Yoast help you edit them.


    Title

    Most important meta tag. Title of page. What is displayed in the tab. Don’t stuff it with keywords to make it look spammy.

    Meta Description

    Sell the value of your webpage. Have congruency with the site though. Can include some keywords.


    Other Factors

    Page Speed

    Affects Ranking.

    Mobile Friendliness

    Affects Ranking.

    Sitemaps

    Use Yoast to keep your Sitemap up to date.


    Backlinks

    • Number of links from other sites * Quality and importance of these websites
    • Why good quality content is often free.
    • Search source code to find nofollow links

    Do follow

    • Default, Endorsement
    • Some Sites allow third party content submissions in exchange for dofollow link

    No follow

    • “I want to link to this site but im not endorsing it, don’t give it any SEO benefit”
    • Often used in comment sections
